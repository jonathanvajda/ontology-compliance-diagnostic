Select one or more ontology files to inspect. Turtle, RDF/XML, JSON-LD, and other supported RDF serializations are parsed locally in your browser. Select options and then run the conformance diagnostic scan.
The dashboard accumulates ontology runs, and selecting a row opens the ontology metadata, ontology checks, and element checks below.

Tip: For keyboard users, standard rows are focusable — press Enter/Space to open details.

Important User Data Notice

By design, the ontology files and reports you work with here stay local to your device. Saved runs use your browser's local storage, and exports created from the Download menu or the Print button are generated on your machine. This site does not upload or store your ontology data on a server.

Because saved runs are browser-local, you should still export the reports you want to keep or share. If you are interested in optional cloud storage in the future, contact Jonathan Vajda (jonathan.vajda [at] gmail.com).
